# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation harassment-free for everyone, regardless of
age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity,
experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Be respectful, accept constructive feedback, focus on what is best for the
community, and show empathy. Harassment, insults, sexualized attention,
publication of private information, and sustained disruption are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's
security/contact facilities. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments,
commits, code, issues, and contributions that violate this code.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
