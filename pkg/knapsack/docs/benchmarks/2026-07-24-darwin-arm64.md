## BoxPacker common-process comparison

The separately recorded comparison at package commit
`bf3fffcfc4ac63efd6f143fd108f4533b3191ac7` used PHP 8.5.8, Go 1.26.5,
BoxPacker 4.2.0 at `4fa822e71109095212a499572822c07bdb7228eb`, one
warm-up, and ten alternating fresh processes per implementation.

| Implementation | Wall p50 | Wall p95 | Solve p50 | Solve p95 | Peak RSS | Quality |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | --- |
| BoxPacker | 35.257 ms | 47.269 ms | 2.070 ms | 2.633 ms | 48,349,184 B | 1 box, 2 items, verified |
| knapsack | 3.675 ms | 5.230 ms | 134.834 us | 194.500 us | 5,849,088 B | 1 box, 2 items, verified |

Wall timing includes process startup, runtime initialization, autoload,
fixture setup, solving, and serialization. It excludes adapter compilation,
Composer installation, and independent verification. Both delayed processes
stopped in 52.6 to 52.8 ms under a 50 ms deadline. Comparable allocation
counts are unavailable across the PHP and Go process boundary, so none are
reported. These measurements describe one tiny common subset and are not a
general speed claim. Complete samples and disclosures are in
`raw/2026-07-24-boxpacker-runtime.json`.
