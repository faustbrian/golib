# Code of conduct

We commit to a harassment-free, welcoming project for everyone. Be respectful,
assume good intent while evaluating technical claims with evidence, accept
constructive feedback, and keep discussion focused on the project.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized
attention, deliberate intimidation, publishing private information, or other
conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers. Maintainers
may edit, remove, reject, or ban contributions or participants when necessary
and will handle reports as confidentially as practical.
