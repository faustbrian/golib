# Goal: Add OpenRPC Specification Decision Evidence

## Objective

Add a complete decision register for OpenRPC behavior discovered after the
original goal and hardening work.

## Required Work

- Implement `docs/specification-decisions.md` under the root specification
  decision contract.
- Reconcile normative prose, meta-schema, examples, errata, extensions, and
  peer behavior; normative prose remains authoritative.
- Cover reference siblings and cycles, method and descriptor uniqueness,
  example validation, schema dialect selection, discovery, extension members,
  canonicalization, composition conflicts, and diff semantics.
- Record alternatives, rationale, compatibility impact, security/resource
  impact, tests, evidence, upstream discussions, and revisit triggers.
- Link every decision to the conformance matrix, implementation, tests,
  interoperability results, documentation, and changelog.

## Completion Criteria

- No prose/meta-schema/example disagreement remains hidden.
- Every observable policy is classified and executable.
- Compliance and interoperability claims are evidence-backed.
