# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We commit to a harassment-free, welcoming community regardless of age, body
size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality,
appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, assume good intent while verifying technical claims, give and
accept constructive feedback, protect private information, and focus conflict
on ideas and evidence rather than people. Harassment, threats, discriminatory
language, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, and publishing
another person's private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the contact method in
`SECURITY.md`, marking the report as a conduct issue. Maintainers may correct,
warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently ban participation according to the
severity and pattern of behavior. Reports are handled as confidentially as
practical, with conflicts of interest recused.

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project in
public spaces.
